> Blockquotes are supported too!

You can mix and match formatting as needed.
- **Bold text**
- _Italic text_
- [Link text](https://example.com)
- ~~Strikethrough text~~
- <u>Underline text</u>
- <mark>Highlighted text</mark>
- Superscript text: E=mc<sup>2</sup>
- <sub>Subscript text</sub>
- <small>Small text</small>
